User story:

Chris would like to upload photos from the camping trip he went on last weekend to his Flickr Photostream

Use case:

Title: Uploading Chris's photos to his Photostream

Scenario: Chris is a computer-savvy amateur photographer. He took some photos on a weekend camping trip that he'd like to share on Flickr.

Usage preconditions:

  • User must have a Flickr account
  • User must be logged in to Flickr account
  • User must have photos to upload

Usage postconditions:

  • Chris's photos are uploaded to his Photostream
  • Chris's photos are tagged and can be discovered by other photographers
  • Chris can receive feedback from fellow photographers

Interaction flow:

  • Chris opens up his browser and enters "flickr.com" in his navbar
  • Server processes the request, returns Flickr homepage
  • Chris clicks on "log in" button
  • Server redirects Chris to Yahoo login page
  • Chris enters his email into the Sign In box
  • Server prompts Chris to enter a password
  • Chris enters password
  • Server redirects Chris back to Flickr homepage
  • Chris clicks on upload button in header of page
  • Server directs Chris to upload page
  • Chris selects the photos he would like to upload from his files
  • Chris changes the titles of and adds relevant tags to each photo
  • Chris now clicks on the "Upload x Photos" button
  • Server redirects Chris to his Photostream page where he can now view his newly uploaded photos

Frequency of use: Once every couple of weeks
