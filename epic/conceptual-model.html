Conceptual Model

Entities and Attributes

USER PROFILE

  • profileId(primary key)
  • profileRealName
  • profileDisplayName
  • profileEmail
  • profileHash
  • profileWebAddress

PHOTO

  • photoId(primary key)
  • photoProfileId(foreign key)
  • photoTitle
  • photoFormat
  • photoSize
  • photoCopyright
  • photoPath

COMMENT

  • commentId(primary key)
  • commentProfileId(foreign key)
  • commentPhotoId(foreign key)
  • commentDate

LIKE

  • likeId(primary key)
  • likePhotoId(foreign key)
  • likeProfileId(foreign key)

Relations:

  • One user can upload many photos(1 to n)
  • One photo can have many comments (1 to n)
  • Many photos can be liked by many profiles (m to n)
